About McKinsol Technologies Pvt. Ltd

McKinsol Technologies Pvt. Ltd (MTPL) is the IT services consulting arm of McKinsol Group. McKinsol Technologies integrates the core expertise and values of the McKinsol companies and its partners to deliver complete business solutions in the areas of SAP Consulting, Managed Application Services, Emerging Technologies, and IT Consulting Services.
McKinsol Technologies Pvt. Ltd is a Global Delivery Center and is ably supported by its skilled workforce across development centers in Noida and Delhi, Virtual offices in Bangalore, Pune, and Hyderabad to enable the delivery of services to more than 16 countries.

Our affiliates NeuVays and Intellicept are focused on Process Automation & Test Automation Services, Emerging Technologies (AI/ML, Blockchain, and IoT) based

Business Application Services, Mobile Applications, Web Technologies, and E-commerce Portals including having in-house over 50 E-commerce portals.

McKinsol Technologies is ISO/IEC 20000-1:2018 Certified, ISO 27001:2013 Certified and ISO 9001:2015 as a testimony of the IT Service, IT Security, and Quality Management of our operations.
This enables us to offer our clients quality, predictable and continuously improving deliverables every time. With over 250 projects across verticals, we endeavor to add value through our services to our customers, partners, and offer our employees a world-class work ecosystem to perform.
