McKinsol Consulting Inc.

McKinsol Consulting, a Premier SAP Silver Partner and a full service consulting organization with a special focus on Retail, Fashion, Consumer Products, Life Sciences, Utilities Industries and Enterprise Information Management (EIM) across industries, was established in 2008 in New Jersey by some of top notch platinum level SAP consultants. During this twelve (12) years of long journey McKinsol has delivered several of SAP ECC, S/4 Fashion, S/4 Retail, ECC Retail, FMS, SAP IS Utilities, Ecommerce Integration and data migration projects successfully to multiple esteemed and continued clients. Many of these clients are large multi-billion dollar firms across continents however few are small but promising companies.

McKinsol’s many of service offerings are across industry verticals such as SAP S/4 HANA Implementation, Data Migration & Data Governance, Analytics, Cloud Hosting, Offshore Based Testing, Custom Development and Application Maintenance Support.

We have several accolades earned in these areas working with multiple prestigious clients.

Although McKinsol is a SAP focused and specialized in niche areas of SAP, however in recent years, McKinsol earned certified partnership with Informatica, Ui Path and Suse Linux also.
